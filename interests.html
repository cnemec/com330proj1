The war

Try to guess which movie for fun. It all started where the last film ended. Almost eerily so as if they wanted to make sure the cast had no time inbetween films, well in universe they obviously all had other projects in real life. The villians were easily able to overpower the heroes taking the lives and knocking out the guards like nothing. When all seemed lost they managed to send away 2 minds in order for them to get the help they need. The film then cuts to follow the journey of a man unsatisfied with their current life wanting to get further along in their true goals. While not being the one saught after they wull be the help needed. At first the man is unwilling to leave they have too many responsibilities at home and a journey frankly scares them right now. Then they realize just how big of a threat they face so they leave the planet with the help of friends they just met that day. Friends who didn't like them at first but eventually see them as worthy allies.
