The importance of history

Now the background of a person is what makes them who they are. Some say it means much more than one's ambitions as it is what causes it all. Their favorite food, their hobbies, their ideal traits for a partner, their ambition these are all caused by ones history.' Learning about ones past is the best way to understand them hence why anthropology isn't treated like a psuedoscience. If you know someones history it is safe to say you have a solid understanding of them.
